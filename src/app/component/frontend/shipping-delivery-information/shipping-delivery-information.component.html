Shipping and Delivery Information

Welcome to www.revtriphotels.com, your trusted source for hotel and accommodation reservations. While we don't offer physical shipping or delivery services for our online platform, we provide essential information and services related to your bookings.

1. Booking Confirmation

1.1. Once you complete your reservation on our website, you will receive a booking confirmation email. This email will contain all the details of your booking, including the hotel or accommodation name, address, check-in and check-out dates, room type, and any additional services you've selected.

2. Electronic Vouchers

2.1. For hotel and accommodation bookings, you will typically receive an electronic voucher via email. This voucher serves as proof of your booking and includes essential details for check-in.

3. Check-In Process

3.1. Upon arrival at the hotel or accommodation, present your electronic voucher and valid identification to the front desk or reception.

4. Booking Changes and Modifications

4.1. If you need to make changes to your booking, such as modifying dates, room types, or guest information, please log in to your account on our website. Follow the provided instructions to request changes. Note that changes may be subject to availability and the policies of the hotel or accommodation.

5. Contact Us

5.1. If you have any questions or need assistance regarding your booking or any related matters, our customer support team is here to help. You can reach us at customer-support@revtriphotels.com, and we'll be glad to assist you.

Please remember that our services primarily focus on facilitating hotel and accommodation reservations. Physical shipping or delivery of products is not part of our offering. For any inquiries related to your booking, reservations, or other services, please refer to the information above or contact our customer support team.
